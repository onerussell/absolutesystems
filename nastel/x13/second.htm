Nastel confirms support for IBM WebSphere MQ version 6.0

Nastel's AutoPilot suite provides visibility into the health of your business services by monitoring the middleware, applications, and transactions.

AutoPilot lets everyone who has a stake in the business integration of their enterprise to access role-based performance views. The role-based views aid the stakeholder to see in an end-to-end transaction environment performance bottlenecks related to their specific area of responsibility. For example, in a transaction scenario in which the Data Center sees a broken wide-area link, if the Middleware Administrator sees a failed WebSphere MQ channel, and the Application Group sees an incomplete transaction, AutoPilot can instantly identify the root cause of failure. The stakeholders can be notified of the problem in the terms they can understand, and can be presented with the required metrics for troubleshooting and preventing any negative impact on business services.
